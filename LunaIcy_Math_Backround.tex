\section{Backround}
Consider the PDE and the ODE
\[
\frac{\partial}{\partial t} T(x,t) = S(x,t) \frac{\partial^2}{\partial x^2} T(x,t), \quad \frac{\partial}{\partial t} S(x,t) = T(x,t) - S(x,t), \quad \forall x,t
\]
Then with symmetric differences
\[
\frac{\partial}{\partial t} T(x,t) = S(x,t) \frac{T(x-h,t)-2 T(x,t)+T(x+h,t)}{h^2} + \mathcal{O}(h^4).
\]
We now discretize the state space $\Omega$ by $[x_0,...,x_{n+1}]$ and define $s_i (t) := S(x_i ,t)$ and $T_i (t) := S_(x_i,t)$ and then get
\[
\frac{d}{dt} \begin{pmatrix}
    T_1 \\
    S_1 \\
    \hdots \\
    T_n \\
    S_n
\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}
    S_1 (t) \frac{T_0(t) - 2 T_1 (t) + T_2 (t) }{h^2} + \mathcal{O}(h^4) \\
    T_1 (t) - S_1 (t) \\
    \hdots \\
    S_n (t) \frac{T_{n-1} (t) - 2 T_n (t) + T_{n+1} (t) }{h^2}+ \mathcal{O}(h^4) \\
    T_n (t) - S_n (t) \\
\end{pmatrix}
\]
which is a system of $2n$ ODEs.
